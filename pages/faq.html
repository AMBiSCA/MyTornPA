FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

We're a passionate two-person team dedicated to creating handy Torn City utilities. MyTornPA stems from our love for the game and community.

Why choose MyTornPA?

Fueled by our love for Torn City, we build reliable, user-friendly tools to enhance your gameplay. MyTornPA is crafted with passion to serve you.

What is MyTornPA?

MyTornPA offers a suite of tools, like Battle Stats Peeper, to enhance your Torn City experience. New users receive a 30-day free trial upon registration. Afterwards, access is 15m monthly or a one-time 500m payment for lifetime use. This supports continued development for the community.

Is MyTornPA affiliated with Torn City or TornStats?

No, MyTornPA is an independent fan project. We use APIs like TornStats (with your key for some tools) but have no official affiliation.

How is my data, like API keys, handled?

Your security is key. API keys are handled client-side (in your browser) for direct service communication (e.g., TornStats) and aren't stored by us. See our Disclaimer/Terms.

How can I get support or report a bug?

Use the 'Report an Issue' link provided below in the footer. We welcome feedback, bug reports, and suggestions to improve MyTornPA! For Donations to keep us running see About Us page.

Thanks for checking out our FAQ! We hope MyTornPA enhances your Torn City experience.
