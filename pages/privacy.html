Privacy Policy

What We Collect & Why:

  • Account: Email for login (Firebase Auth).
  • Profile: Your display name, Torn Profile ID, and Torn API Key (stored in Firebase Firestore).
  • Torn Data (via your API Key): We fetch your Torn ID, Name, Faction ID, stats, and faction details directly from the official Torn City API. Your API Key is stored securely and used by your browser for direct API calls; we do not share it.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous, aggregated data on feature use to improve MyTornPA.
  • Chat Messages: Messages sent via in-app chat (Faction, Private) are stored in Firebase Firestore and visible to chat participants.

How We Use Your Information:

  • To manage your account and provide tools (e.g., displaying your Torn stats, tracking activity, enabling chat).
  • To improve MyTornPA's features and security.

Data Storage & Security:

  • Data is stored securely in Google's Firebase Firestore, protected by encryption.
  • Your Torn API Key is securely stored and used only by your browser for Torn API calls; we do not share or sell your personal data.

Third-Party Services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and database.
  • Torn City API: For fetching game data.
  • PayPal: For optional donations (we do not collect payment info directly).

Your Rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data (via profile settings or contact us).
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., by removing your API key).

Policy Changes:

  • We may update this policy; significant changes will be posted here. Continued use implies acceptance.
